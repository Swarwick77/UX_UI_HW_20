Problem Statement

People wanting to adopt pets or volunteer encounter difficulties locating and processing information on the current website, leading to frustration. How might we help them find what they are looking for and complete the tasks they are trying to accomplish more efficiently?

Original Website Home Page

Our users found the home page cluttered and outdated.

Our Redesigned Home Page

Our users found it easier to navigate and find the information they needed quickly.

Solution

We redesigned the Catering to Cats and Dogs website in a way that makes it more user friendly and inviting. We got rid of redundant and useless features within the original website to make it less cluttered and less confusing.
